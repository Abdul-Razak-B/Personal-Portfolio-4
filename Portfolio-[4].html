About Me

I am Abdul Razak, a dedicated educator, researcher, and web developer based in Daharki, Sindh. Currently, I serve as a Lecturer and Subject Specialist in Computer Science at Sona Public School & College in Mirpur Mathelo, building upon my previous experience as a Junior School Teacher at Shah Latif Model High School. My academic foundation includes a BS in Computer Science from Shah Abdul Latif University and a Bachelor of Education (BEd) from Ammara College Ghotki.


As an MPhil Scholar at the National College of Business Administration & Economics (NCBA&E) Rahim Yar Khan, my research focuses on the Design and Implementation of a Web-Oriented Research Recommendation System (DIWORRS), with a specialized interest in Cloud Computing and Zero Trust Architecture. Beyond academia, I have developed several digital solutions, including the Chill Time Cafeteria Management System, the Markaz E Zaiqa food portal, and a Library Management System. Born on April 1, 2001, into the Bhutto caste, I am the son of Abdul Hakeem, a Head Master, and I remain committed to advancing technical education and system design within my community.
